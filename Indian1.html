Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer also known as cottage cheese is a by product of milk and is widely used in India to prepare many main course dishes. Paneer butter masala is authentic dish prepared by the using all the robust spices from all over India and is eaten along with Breads or rice

Paneer Butter Masala recipe

    Step by step process

  • Step 1:

    • >First soak cashew-nuts and grind it to a paste with little water and keep aside.

  • Step 2:

    • >Grind the tomatoes in a blender and keep aside it will be about 1 1/2 cups tomato puree. >Onion, ginger and garlic either chop them finely or mince them in a food processor.

  • Step 3:

    • >Then we need to heat this puree by adding some water to it. And boil mixture for about 20 minutes

  • Step 4:

    • >During the cooking process of the puree, we simultaneously should butter fry the paneer

  • Step 5:

    • >After the mixture is enough cooked we need to add the cashew paste and paneerpieces and leave them to cook again for another 10 minutes.

  • Step 6:

    • >After the given time of 10 minutes is completed we can see that the gravy has hardened and the paneer pieces are cooked

  • Step 7:

    • Now the curry is ready to be served hot.
